How it plays

Five life points. Weapons one-shot enemies. Mushrooms grow you Mario-style.

❤️

Life points

Start with 5 hearts. Ghosts, skulls, robots and snowmen all cost one life on touch.

🗡️

Weapons

Sword, hammer and water gun one-shot any enemy. Bare hands take two hits.

🍄

Mushrooms

Raise your max life by 2 and trail behind you. A hit pops the tail mushroom instead of your life.

🪶

Feather flight

Picking up a feather lifts you into the air for 3 seconds — invulnerable while flying.

🎙️

Voice

Say "poo poo doo doo" for a bomb, "kaka kaka" and birds drop 💩, "I'm a peacock… fly" takes you skyward.

⭐

Winning

Collect every ⭐ on the grid before the timer runs out, or outlive the round if none are placed.
